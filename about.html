Transforming Challenges into Opportunities: A Global Tech Career

From Venezuela to the Frontiers of Technology

Born in San Cristobal, Venezuela, my journey began with a passion for engineering and a drive to make a difference. At 17, I embarked on an international adventure, moving to Gainesville, Florida to pursue Mechanical Engineering at the University of Florida, specializing in Instrumentation and Measurements.

This foundation set the stage for an exhilarating start to my career at the University of Florida's Hurricane Research center. Here, I honed my skills in instrumentation and real-time data acquisition, chasing hurricanes Dennis, Katrina, and Wilma. This experience not only sharpened my technical abilities but also instilled a deep appreciation for the power of data in understanding and mitigating natural phenomena.

Global Impact in Oil and Gas

My career then took me across the globe as a Field Engineer for Schlumberger, where I worked in Integrated Project Management and Rig Management. Operating in Oman and Venezuela, both onshore and offshore, I gained invaluable experience in Oil and Gas Drilling and Workover Operations. This role taught me the importance of adaptability, precision, and leadership in high-stakes environments.

Around this time, I made the significant decision to move to Canada, embarking on a new chapter in both my personal and professional life. Transitioning into Quality Control and Assurance, I oversaw critical inspections of drilling equipment, further developing my eye for detail and commitment to excellence. This experience laid the groundwork for my next role as a Quality Specialist, where I spent six years focusing on root cause analysis, incident management, and continuous improvement for companies designing and manufacturing Oil and Gas equipment.

Pioneering in Tech and Data

Recognizing the growing importance of technology in industry, I pivoted to application and automation development in the financial sector. This role allowed me to merge my engineering background with cutting-edge software solutions, creating efficient systems that streamlined operations.

My journey then led me to data engineering, where I applied my diverse skill set to perform data analytics and business intelligence for equipment in the oil and gas, farming, and mining industries. Today, I continue to push the boundaries of what's possible with data as a Data Engineer at a leading financial institution.

A Unique Blend of Skills

My expertise spans a wide range of technologies and methodologies, including Azure, Fabric, Synapse Analytics, Oracle, SQL Server, Python, Power BI, Tableau, Data Factory, Databricks, Rust, DuckDB, Power Platform, Dynamics, Docker, Javascript, and Plotly. This technical proficiency is complemented by my experience in Incident Management, Root Cause Analysis, Continuous Improvement, Quality Control, Project Management, and more.

From Oil and Gas to Finance, from Manufacturing to Inspection, my career has been defined by adaptability, continuous learning, and a commitment to excellence. I bring a unique perspective that combines hands-on field experience with cutting-edge data and technology skills, making me a valuable asset to any forward-thinking organization.

Ready for the Next Challenge

As I look to the future, I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead. My diverse background, technical expertise, and passion for innovation position me to tackle complex challenges and drive meaningful change. I am eager to bring my unique blend of skills and experiences to a company, where I can contribute to groundbreaking projects and help shape the future of industry and technology.
